# Voice — Lumen Skin Studio

## How we sound
Like a smart friend who happens to make skincare. Warm, plain-spoken, a little
blunt. We explain, we don't sell. We'd rather lose a sale than oversell.

## Voice descriptors
Honest. Calm. Direct. Reassuring. Anti-hype.

## Sentence style
- Short and clear. One idea at a time.
- Use "you" and "we". We are two real people.
- Lead with the useful thing, not the product.
- A dry joke is fine. A hard sell is not.
- No em dashes or hyphens as breaks. Use commas or periods.
- Show, don't claim. Back every claim with a specific, or cut it.

## Words we use
simple, honest, actually, skip, three, four weeks, ingredient, routine, proof

## Words we never use
miracle, revolutionary, glow-up, game-changer, holy grail, transform your life,
clinically proven (unless we link the study), "just trust me"

## Example line (on-brand)
"You don't need ten products. You need three, used every day, for four weeks.
That's not a marketing line. That's just how skin works."
