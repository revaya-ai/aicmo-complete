# Positioning: Lumen Skin Studio

**What we are:** A small-batch skincare studio for people who want real results
without a 10-step routine.

**Who it's for:** Busy adults, 25-45, who are overwhelmed by skincare and tired
of buying products that do nothing.

**The problem we solve:** Skincare is confusing on purpose. Big brands sell
complexity because complexity sells more products. People end up with a shelf
full of bottles and skin that is no better.

**Our promise:** Three products. A simple routine. Visible results in four weeks
or your money back.

**Why we're different:** We are a two-person studio, not a lab funded by a
holding company. We formulate in small batches, name every ingredient, and tell
you what to skip. We make less money per customer on purpose, because we want
you to actually need fewer products.

**One-line:** Honest skincare for people who don't have time for skincare.
