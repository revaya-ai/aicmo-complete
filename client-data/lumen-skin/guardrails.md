# Guardrails: Lumen Skin Studio

Hard rules for every piece of content. The Brand QC station checks against these.

## Claims
- No medical claims. We are skincare, not treatment. Never say "cures",
  "treats acne", or imply we replace a dermatologist.
- No "clinically proven" unless we link the actual study.
- Every result claim must be tied to the four-week guarantee, not a guarantee of
  a specific outcome.

## Tone
- Never use hype words (see voice.md "never" list).
- Never create fake urgency ("only 3 left!", fake countdowns).
- Never shame the reader's skin or current routine.

## Legal / safety
- Always mention patch-testing for new actives.
- Never give individual medical advice in comments. Point to a professional.
- No claims about pregnancy safety without a clear "ask your doctor".

## Brand integrity
- Always name key ingredients. Never hide a formula.
- Never imply we are bigger than we are. We are two people.
- Never copy a competitor's exact angle. Be honest, be ours.
